Career history

Charles has had a varied career with business administration being the underlying theme.

He started off in the legal profession - training as a corporate finance lawyer at Allen & Overy and qualifying as a solicitor in 1996.

In 1997 he moved to the retail industry and spent 2 years working as a sales assistant. This gave him a better understanding of how businesses operate on a day-to-day level.

In 1999 he moved to the accountancy profession. He trained with Mazars and then went to KPMG where he was an assistant manager in audit.

From 2003-15 Charles was an in-house accountant - mainly at advertising, hotel & PR companies. His most significant role was 5 years as the chief accountant and group financial controller at Omnicom Media Group UK.

Charles graduated in Economics from Edinburgh University and his A level subjects were Maths, Physics and Chemistry.
